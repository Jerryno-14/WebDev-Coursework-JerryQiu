Sara's Love for Traditional Cooking

About Sara's Cooking

Introduction

Sara, from Brooklyn, NY, with roots in Trinidad and Tobago, loves cooking doubles.
She learned from her grandmother and uses chickpeas and tamarind sauce.

Her dishes bring family together!

Make a double

  • Order to make a double
    1. Mix flour and water for dough.
    2. Fry dough until golden.
    3. Cook curry chana with spices.
    4. Top with tamarind sauce.
  • Ingredients
    1. Flour
    2. Chickenpeas
    3. Tamarind
    4. Curry powder
